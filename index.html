VacationLive

VacationLive is an Android application designed to help users organize and manage their vacations and excursions in one convenient place. The application allows users to create vacations, track associated excursions, and receive important notifications related to their travel plans.

Key features include:

  • Create, view, edit, and delete vacation plans
  • Manage excursions associated with each vacation
  • Receive notifications for important vacation dates
  • Store and manage data locally on the device
  • Simple and user-friendly interface
